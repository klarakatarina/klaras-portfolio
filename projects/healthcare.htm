Service design - healthcare app

The scope of this project was to improve patients first point of contact with healthcare in the Stockholm region. As a group we were expected to develop insights, design services and suggest proposals of how to plan and organize healthcare services. The customer segment researched was young adults, and three behavioral groups were defined: The Netflix mentality, the Inexperienced user and the Avoid seeking care.

Customer journey mapping

Customer journey mapping was used for the three groups to gather insights and understand problem areas.

Three type of trigger material was developed, tested and analyzed. The second one was the most successful at reaching the design criteria and was further developed into the final concept- CareTracker.
